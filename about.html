About Eberesport

Eberesport is a professional gaming and entertainment company headquartered in Norrköping, Sweden. Founded in 2016, Eberesport has embodied competitive esports excellence and the best of gaming culture over the last half-decade. With its championship teams and innovative gaming lifestyle content, Eberesport has amassed a respectable trophy cabinet all throughout their years as an organization.

Eberesport boasts a VCT partnered VALORANT squad, a World Championship Rocket League team, and an Apex Legends squad with numerous national and international titles under its belt. Additionally, Eberesport is home to the San Francisco Shock, the org's Overwatch League team, who is the winningest and most popular Overwatch team of all time, having won 2019, 2020 back-to-back Grand Finals as well as the 2022 runner up.

Eberesport's content channels span YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, T witter, Facebook and multiple Snapchat Discover shows. Eberesport's social gaming brand, Full Squad Gaming, has become an instant powerhouse amassing millions of followers on TikTok and YouTube. Eberesport content channels currently reach over 70M uniques a month and over 5 million views a day!

Eberesport also has opened the Eberesport Castle in Downtown Los Angeles. This innovative studio / play space is a Fantasy Factory for gamers featuring giant creative installations, studio space, scrim / stream areas and general craziness.
